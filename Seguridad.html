SECURITY

At LTB, protecting your personal and financial information is our highest priority. Our security protocols ensure that your data is handled with the utmost care, confidentiality, and compliance with industry standards.

Data Traffic

  • Secure Communication: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3. This ensures that all information shared with us remains confidential and is protected from unauthorized access.
  • Data Types Transmitted: We handle a range of data types, including personal identification data, account details, and transactional information. LTB only transmits the minimum necessary information to execute the requested financial service securely.

Infrastructure

  • Data Centers: LTB operates through secure data centers that are compliant with the highest industry standards, such as ISO 27001 and SOC 2. These centers are equipped with multiple layers of physical and digital security to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Cloud Storage and Backup: All data is stored in secure cloud environments, with regular backups and redundancy to ensure data availability and resilience in case of unforeseen incidents.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to our systems. Access is granted based on roles and responsibilities, and is continuously monitored and reviewed.

Security Standards

  • Encryption Standards: LTB implements end-to-end encryption and hashing methods to protect data both in transit and at rest.
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security audits, including vulnerability assessments and penetration tests, to ensure our defenses remain effective.
  • Compliance: LTB adheres to financial and data protection standards such as GDPR, PCI DSS, etc.

Data Handling

  • Data Collection: We only collect data that is strictly necessary for account creation, transaction processing, and customer support.
  • Data Processing: Personal data is processed under strict guidelines to ensure confidentiality and security. All data handling complies with applicable data protection laws and industry regulations.
  • Data Retention: Data is retained only for as long as it is necessary for legal, financial, and operational purposes. When no longer needed, it is securely deleted.
